Advantages of Artificial Intelligence

    1. Minimal human response: In environments where human life is more likely to be at risk, AI-powered systems are the best-fit solutions. Space exploration, defense operations such as bomb defusing, workplaces characterized by intense heat, mineral mining etc. are few examples of such scenarios.

    2. Faster and Accurate: The performance of well-trained AI-enabled applications dramatically reduces the possibility of human errors creeping into. These AI versions proved to be faster, particularly in the field of scientific research and time-consuming tasks, at computationally-cost tasks. With proper AI, most routine, irrelevant, and repetitive tasks can be automated, pushing the technology to boost operational efficiencies.
