Overview

Artificial Intelligence is the ability to design smart machines or develop self-learning software applications that imitate the human mind 's traits such as reasoning , problem-solving, planning, optimum decision-making, sensory percussions etc. Artificial intelligent approaches' ability to outperform human actions in terms of knowledge discovery has attracted the attention of business and research communities around the world and this field of study has seen rapid progress over the past two decades.
